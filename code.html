How Blackbox AI Works

Get from idea to production in just a few simple steps

1

Connect Your Data

Easily import your datasets from various sources including cloud storage, databases, or upload directly to our platform. 

from blackbox import Dataset
dataset = Dataset.connect("postgresql://user:pass@host/db")
dataset.load_table("customer_data")
2

Build Your Model

Use our intuitive interface or code directly with our Python SDK to create and train your AI models. 

from blackbox import Model
model = Model.create("xgboost")
model.train(dataset, target="churn")
3

Deploy & Monitor

Deploy your model with one click and monitor its performance in real-time with our comprehensive analytics. 

deployment = model.deploy(
    name="customer_churn_predictor",
    scale=10,
    region="us-east-1"
)
